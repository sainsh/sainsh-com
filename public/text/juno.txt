Juno<br>
Juno teaches strength in friendship and companionship <br>
and that one should always be prepared to protect those weaker than oneself. <br>
She is strong and compasionate but can be a stern teacher. <br>
She adores freedom but knows the responsibility of strength and abhores slavery of any kind. <br>
She helps those who fight for a just cause but disdaines those who trick others into fighting for false pretences<br>
Domains:  community, fire, glory, good, healing, knowledge, liberation, nobility, protection, ruins, sun, travel, war<br>
