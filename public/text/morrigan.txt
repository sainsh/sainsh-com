Morrigan<br>
Morrigan teaches about the beauty and strength of nature <br>
and that one should live in harmony with nature.<br>
She is strong and indepedent but sometimes too chaotic for civilised societies. <br>
She adores nature and all beings of the world <br>
and she abhores anything unnatural like evil outsiders, aberrations, and the undead.<br>
She nutures the weak but favores the strong, since that is the way of nature<br>
Domains:  air, animal, chaos, earth, healing, liberation, luck, plant, scalykind, strength, sun, trickery, vermin, water, weather<br>
Favored weapon type: ranged piercing<br>
